About Satoshi Nakamoto

Satoshi Nakamoto is the anonymous creator of Bitcoin Cash. He is the mind behind the design and operation of the Bitcoin protocol, a self-sustaining incentivized network and the creator of the first client to run on the network, Bitcoin Core.

His original protocol and design have stood the test of time, proving that it is possible to have a completely electronic form of money without a central issuer and that does not require intermediaries to function properly.

Satoshi Nakamoto has mainly spoken out through mailing lists and discussion forums (mainly BitcoinTalk), explaining the model, design and operation of his invention and intentions for its long-term evolution. It is from this that we know that Satoshi promoted the use of Bitcoin directly, settling transactions on the blockchain and with a free market approach, i.e. without intervening in issues concerning block size, processing capacity, long-term incentives and secondary uses of the network.

At the same time, Satoshi has also been in favor of Bitcoin replacing other centralized digital payment systems at the base layer, which perform millions of transactions year after year, and advocates the removal of these financial entities, allowing an electronic cash system with low fees and ease of use.
